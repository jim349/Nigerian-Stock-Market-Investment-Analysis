# Nigerian Stock Market Investment Performance Analysis (2020–2025)

## Project Overview

This project evaluates the financial performance of six Nigerian listed companies between 2020 and 2025 using financial ratios, profitability metrics, and investment indicators.

The objective is to identify companies with the strongest long-term investment potential through data analysis and visualization.

---

## Business Questions

- Which company recorded the highest Return on Equity (ROE)?
- Which company generated the highest Earnings Per Share (EPS)?
- Which company paid the highest Dividend Per Share (DPS)?
- Which company achieved the strongest profit growth?
- Which company experienced the highest asset growth?
- Which company provides the best overall investment opportunity?

---

## Key Insights

- Presco recorded the highest Overall Investment Score.
- MTN generated the highest Return on Equity.
- Seplat achieved the highest EPS and DPS.
- GTCO recorded the strongest annual profit growth.
- Agriculture and Oil & Gas companies outperformed Banking on growth metrics.

---

## Recommendations

- Consider balanced investment across sectors.
- Prioritize companies with strong profitability and sustainable growth.
- Evaluate both dividend performance and long-term financial health before investing.
